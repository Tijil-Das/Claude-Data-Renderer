Where do I get an export?
  1. In Claude, click your initials (bottom left) → Settings
  2. Go to the Privacy section
  3. Click Export data and confirm
  4. Anthropic emails you a download link within a few minutes
  5. Download the archive and drop it above — no need to unzip it first
